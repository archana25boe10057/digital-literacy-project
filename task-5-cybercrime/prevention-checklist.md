## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown UPI “collect requests”  
- Always verify links before clicking (avoid suspicious URLs)  
- Enable two-factor authentication on all important accounts  
- Use strong, unique passwords for different platforms  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Regularly update apps and devices for security patches
