## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

### Do’s 
- Maintain a professional profile picture and bio on public accounts  
- Share academic achievements, projects, or relevant articles  
- Use privacy settings to control who can view your posts  
- Fact-check information before sharing to avoid misinformation  
- Be respectful and polite in all online interactions  

### Don’ts 
- Don’t post offensive, inappropriate, or discriminatory content  
- Avoid sharing personal passwords, financial info, or sensitive data  
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling  
- Avoid oversharing personal life details publicly  
- Don’t plagiarize content
