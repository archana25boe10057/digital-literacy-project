## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic (Reflection)

For this task, I created a one-page digital literacy infographic using Canva. The design focuses on key areas such as the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and maintaining a professional online presence. I selected a clean layout with icons and short points to make the information easy to understand and visually engaging.

While designing, I found it interesting to organize complex ideas into simple visuals that can quickly communicate the message to students. One challenge I faced was deciding how much content to include without overcrowding the design. I had to balance clarity with completeness.

Overall, this task helped me understand how visual communication can be used effectively to spread awareness, especially on important topics like digital responsibility and online safety.
